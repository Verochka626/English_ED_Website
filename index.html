Goodbye

This is the most straightforward and formal way to bid farewell(it's suitable for almost any situation, though it can sometimes feel a bit distant)

See you later/See ya

This one commonly used among friends or colleagues.

So long

It is an old-fashioned way to say goodbye

Catch you later

You can use this expression when talk with people you have known for a long time

Take care

A wish for the other person's well-being

Farewell

An archaic way of saying goodbye, it's often used in formal speeches or in writing, and it carries a sense of finality
